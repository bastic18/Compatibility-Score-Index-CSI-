About Réseau

Background - CSI

During this technological era, a number of advancements have been made to facilitate ease of meeting persons for the sole benefit of forming partnerships. It was with this in mind where the development of Compatibility Score Index stemmed.
It is our passion to go beyond the outer features of a person and focus on his/her unique attributes for the purpose of providing an accurate and yet efficient system.
Our system seeks to present to the user a set of compatible persons based on Age, Gender, Height, Hobby, Ethnicity, Personality Type, Leadership Style, Education Level and Occupation ; instead of focusing solely on the outer appearances to determine compatibility. It is desired that this will assist in breaking down the barriers of accessible matching for future generations.
From CSI, came Réseau, the web application that will assist users with all these aspects.

Organize Teams

An organizer is an individual who organizes groups. Organizers are able to create sets and form groups within them. These groups are generated from a list of compatible persons or even non-compatible persons if they so desire.

After the creation of these groups, the organizer can then view the groups as mentioned above, as well as manage each group by being able to make changes to each if necessary.

Build Friendships

A regular user enters the necessary information about themselves in order to generate a list of other users who fall under compatible and/or non-compatible in relation to them. From this list the user may pin/add that user to their list of friends as well as view the breakdown of compatibility among those persons.

In addition, this user will aslo be allowed to join sets formed by organizers once given the 'group code' and be placed in groups.
